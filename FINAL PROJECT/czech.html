    • CZECH REPUBLIC

    While in college, I studied abroad in Prague, Czech Republic and couldn't have been happier with my decision. Prague is an amazing and gorgeous Eastern European city, totally preserved and phyically unharmed from WWII, which is very rare for Europe. The country has so much culture, beer and beautiful cities.

    What to Expect

    Language

    In the Czech Republic, they speak the wonderful language of Czech. This may be one of the uglier languages I Have encountered in my travels, however, after being mandated to take Czech 101 for 4 months straight, I grew to love it. However, in Prague, I woudl stick to English. Eastern Europeans are known to be not so friendly, so they would just appreciate you speaking in English and not wasting their time trying to decipher the language.

    Currency

    The currency in the Czech Republic is the Koruna (crown). Around 25 crowns equal a dollars, aka Prague is ridiculously cheap! Beer is known to be cheaper than water everywhere as it is around $.75, seriously. Everything else is ridiculously cheap as well.
